Cookie Monster Smoothie Bowl

Description

This protein-packed smoothie bowl is the ultimate for getting in an extra serving or two of protein and keeping you energized throughout the day.

The perfect balance of healthy fats, invigorating carbs, and hunger-satisfying protein, this smoothie bowl will please your taste buds and provide your body a plethora of vital nutrients to make it through the day. It’s full of flavor and easy to make, you’ll never go back to your plain protein shakes again!

Nutritional Facts: 519 kcal, 81g carbs, 27g protein, 13g fat

Ingredients

Smoothie:
  • 1/3 cup almond
  • 1 scoop Twix protein powder
  • 200g frozen banana
  • 135g frozen peeled zucchini
Toppings:
  • 1/2 banana
  • 3 strawberries
  • 1 pouch Made Good soft mini chocolate chip cookies

Steps

  1. Scale ingredients in blender jug and wait 5-10 minutes.
  2. Blend until smooth with the tamper (vitamix speed 5-6).
  3. Pour into a chilled ceramic bowl.
  4. Add toppings of choice.
