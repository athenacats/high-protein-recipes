Protein Frapuccino

Description

This Homemade Protein Coffee Frappuccino could not be easier… or tastier!

Confession time. I have a bit of an obsession with coffee shop Coffee Light Frappuccino drinks, blended with soy. Yes, I’m well aware that even the light versions are loaded with empty calories.

It’s time for a healthier homemade version, I can whip up any time I’m hankering for the frothy deliciousness. I’m craving a lightly sweetened, irresistibly creamy, easy frappe that’s healthy enough to enjoy for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 5 pumps (40 g) of sugar free syrup
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1tbs Nescafé instant espresso powder
  • 30 g Chocolate Optimum Nutrition protein powder
  • 12 ice cubes (250-280 g)

Steps

  1. Add all items to a high speed blender
  2. Blend it for 2 min or until smooth
  3. Pour into cup or travel thermos and enjoy
